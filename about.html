About

Industrial Surplus Hub is a division of Jameson Machining Inc., focused on responsibly reselling high-quality tooling and equipment from our precision grinding operations and partner networks.

We specialize in Kapp Niles electroplated CBN gear grinding wheels, diamond/CBN tooling, and select machinery. We ship across Canada, the U.S., and internationally.
